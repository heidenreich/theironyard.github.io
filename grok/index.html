What the fock is Grok?

Grok is an invitation. Come to one of the best small cities in the nation. Talk, listen, eat and drink with world class people asking a ton of hard questions, digging into great issues, and making headway on some decent real-world solutions.

It’s a three day event sans the how-to sessions from industry leaders. We think a 10 minute session of hard questions from a new face is as good as a lock-tight answer from an old pro. We started Grok because we wanted to learn big, and go big. Here’s a peek at last year:

This upcoming year we’ve secured the best venues in Greenville, short talks from unique people outside the web and design industry, and some activities that will blow last year out of the water.

Stay tuned for more information and a schedule soon.

Thanks for making Grok 2013 incredible

Grok is amazing because of the people who attend and the honest conversations they have. We’re thankful for old friends and new who continue to challenge the way we think about our work, year after year. We’re also thankful for good southern food, kickball, laser tag, and beer.

—The Grok Team

Grok is about Greenville

We throw Grok here because we are totally nuts for this place. Beer flows in the streets and the buildings are made from Belgian chocolate. Ok, maybe not, but it might as well be true because that’s how we feel about it. Once you see Greenville for yourself, we think you’ll have second thoughts about leaving.
