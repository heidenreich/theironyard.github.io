Where It All Started

Nestled in the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Greenville combines traditional southern charm, stunning natural beauty, and nationally-respected tech and design communities to create one of America's hottest emerging destinations and fastest-growing cities.

Greenville is also the birthplace of The Iron yard. The home to our HQ, our Greenville location paved the way for our first accelerator program, first intensive Academy courses, and first kids classes.

City Highlights

Greenville offers a plethora of things guaranteed to keep all types of enthusiasts busy. Whether you want to take a leisurely bike ride down the Swamp Rabbit Trail, catch a show at The Peace Center, or sample some of the amazing cuisine the local restaurants have to offer, this town does not disappoint.
