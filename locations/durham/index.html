Durham, NC

Welcome to The Iron Yard Academy powered by Smashing Boxes, Durham’s premier boot camp style code school. We are building better developers to take on the Triangle’s technology economy.

This school represents a unique partnership between a nationally respected code school and an enterprising technology shop, bringing education directly into the market place so that learning and real world experience happen simultaneously.

To connect students directly with potential employers, Academy classes are held in the American Underground, in close proximity to some of the Triangle’s most promising tech start-ups.

What we offer in Durham

Intensive code school

We offer all of our intensive Academy courses in Durham. Our grads get jobs with amazing local companies as well as awesome shops around the country.

Front End Engineering — Learn to build web apps with HTML, CSS & JavaScript.

Rails Engineering — Learn to build full-stack apps with Ruby on Rails.

Mobile Engineering — Learn to build iOS apps using Xcode and Objective-C.

Kids Classes

We believe in investing in the local tech economy for the long-run, and that means impacting the next generation.

Students in our intensive courses are required to volunteer, teach and run our Kids programs as a part of their involvement in the Academy.
