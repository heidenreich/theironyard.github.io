Spartanburg, SC

Originally known for its revolutionary roots, Spartanburg is reviving what once was one of the nation’s busiest railway hubs. Art, music and a generally creative culture combine with connections to some top players in the healthcare industry to make Spartanburg an amazing place for Digital Health companies to plant and grow.
