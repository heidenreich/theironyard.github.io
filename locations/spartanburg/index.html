Spartanburg, SC

Originally known for its revolutionary roots, Spartanburg is reviving what once was one of the nations’ busiest railway hubs. Art, music and other creative culture, combined with connections to some top players in the healthcare industry make it an amazing place to for Digital Health companies to plant and grow.
